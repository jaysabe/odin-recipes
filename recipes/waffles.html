Cathy's Gluten-free Oatmeal Waffles

Description

Cathy's Gluten-Free Oatmeal Waffles are a delightful twist on the classic breakfast favorite, offering a wholesome and inclusive option for those with gluten sensitivities. This recipe seamlessly combines the heartiness of oats with the light and crisp texture of waffles, creating a delicious and nutritious breakfast treat. The gluten-free aspect doesn't compromise on flavor or fluffiness, making these waffles a crowd-pleaser for everyone at the breakfast table. With a perfect balance of sweetness and a hint of nuttiness from the oats, Cathy's creation adds a touch of warmth and comfort to your morning routine, proving that gluten-free options can be just as indulgent and satisfying.

Preheat the waffle iron and grease it. Combine oats and almond milk, then mix in egg yolk and butter. Beat egg white until stiff peaks form. Stir brown sugar and baking powder into the oat mixture, and gently fold in beaten egg white. Scoop 1/2 cup batter onto the preheated iron, spread, and cook for about 5 minutes until steaming stops. Repeat with the remaining batter. Enjoy your gluten-free oatmeal waffles with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1 ½ cups gluten-free rolled oats
  • ¾ cup almond milk
  • 1 egg, separated, or egg replacement
  • 3 tablespoons melted vegan butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions. Grease with cooking spray.
  2. Combine oats and almond milk in a large bowl.
  3. Whisk egg yolk and butter together in a small bowl; stir into oat mixture.
  4. Beat egg white in a glass, metal, or ceramic bowl until stiff peaks form or prepare egg replacement.
  5. Stir brown sugar and baking powder into the oat mixture. Fold beaten egg white / replacement gently into the batter.
  6. Scoop 1/2 cup batter onto the preheated iron and spread to the edges. Close iron and cook until iron stops steaming, about 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
