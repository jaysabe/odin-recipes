Overnight Vegan Cinnamon Rolls

Description

Indulge in the irresistible charm of Overnight Vegan Cinnamon Rolls, a delectable twist on the classic sweet treat. This recipe offers a plant-based twist to the beloved morning delight, ensuring a compassionate and delightful start to your day. The magic unfolds as the dough, crafted with vegan ingredients, rests and rises overnight, allowing the flavors to deepen and the texture to achieve the perfect softness. A heavenly blend of cinnamon and sugar generously coats each layer, promising a swirl of warmth and sweetness in every bite. Come morning, these rolls are ready to be baked to golden perfection, filling your kitchen with the enticing aroma of cinnamon and anticipation for the delightful feast that awaits.

To craft these vegan wonders, a luscious mixture of plant-based ingredients such as almond or soy milk, vegan butter, and a hint of maple syrup contributes to the dough's richness. As you roll out the dough and generously sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar blend, it becomes a canvas for flavor exploration. The overnight rest ensures that the flavors meld seamlessly, resulting in a batch of soft, pillowy rolls that are not only vegan-friendly but also an exquisite homage to the timeless joy of cinnamon rolls. Enjoy them fresh out of the oven or with a drizzle of vegan cream cheese icing for a truly decadent experience that proves plant-based baking can be just as heavenly.

Ingredients

    Dough:

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • ⅓ cup vegan margarine (such as Earth Balance®)
  • 6 tablespoons water
  • 3 tablespoons dry egg replacer (such as Bob's Red Mill®)
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 3 cups unbleached or all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

    • FIlling:

  • ½ cup vegan margarine (such as Earth Balance®), melted
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste
  • ½ cup maple syrup (optional)

    • Glaze:

  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine almond milk and margarine for dough in a large microwave-safe bowl; microwave on high power for 1 1/2 minutes.
  2. Combine water and egg replacer in a bowl; mix well. Let sit for 1 minute to thicken.
  3. Meanwhile, add sugar and yeast to the warm milk mixture and stir to combine. Mix in egg substitute. Add 3 cups flour and salt; mix with your hands until well combined. Add more flour (up to 1 cup) and knead until dough is no longer sticky. Cover bowl with a towel and let rest in a warm place for 1 hour.
  4. Punch down dough and roll on a lightly floured surface into a 12x16-inch rectangle.
  5. Spread melted margarine over the dough and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon; smooth filling ingredients with a spoon. Starting at the long edge, roll dough into a log and cut into 12 rolls. (For bigger rolls, roll from the short edge.) Place rolls in a greased pan, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight.
  6. Remove rolls from the refrigerator, uncover, and allow to warm for 30 to 60 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  8. Bake rolls in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
  9. When rolls are almost finished, mix confectioners' sugar, water, and vanilla for glaze in a bowl. Spread on warm rolls.
